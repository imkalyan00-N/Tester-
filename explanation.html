Module 3: Body as an Instrument

STEP 01: The Basic Relation & Table
MODULE 3: The body as an Instrument of the self (“I” Being the Seer, Doer and Enjoyer)

There is a relation between the self and body that body act as an instrument of self. Whatever self thinks body performs it physically. Body does not decide itself. We can verify this by the following proposals.

Table 4.1: Explore the following proposals
S. No | I | Body
1 | I am | My body is
2 | I want to live | Body is used as an instrument (of ‘I’)
3 | I want to live with continuous happiness | For nurture of body----food
| | For protection of body----clothing, shelter etc.,
| | For right utilization of body--- instruments/equipment etc.
| | Are needed as physical facilities.
4 | My (I) program for continuous happiness is to understand and to live in harmony at all four levels. | Production, protection and right utilization of physical facilities is part of my program
5 | I am the seer, doer and enjoyer | Body is an instrument

🏮 Body is just an Instrument (Keelubomma)

Self ('I') ki mariyu Body ki unna relation enti ante.. Body anedi just oka Vahanam (Instrument) lanti di. Lopalunna 'I' em alochistundo, em decide chestundo.. Body daanni physical ga chestundi anthe!

Body ki swantham ga decide cheskune power ledu (steering driver chethilone untundi). Paina Table lo chupinchinattu.. "Nenu eppudu santhosham ga undali anukuntanu (I), danikosam naa body ki tindi, battalu pedatanu (Body)". Body anedi just nenu vaadukune oka tool matrame.

STEP 02: I am vs Body is
1. ‘I’ am, the ‘Body’ is:
  • ‘I’ (self) knows I exist. I am a conscious/sentient entity.
  • The ‘Body’ doesn’t know. It is a material entity.
2. ‘I’ want to live, the ‘Body’ is my instrument:
  • ‘I’ and my ‘Body’ co-exist with each other.
  • ‘I’ takes decisions and the ‘Body’ acts accordingly.

🏮 Nenu unnanu (Conscious) vs Na Body undi (Material)

1. Nenu & Naa Body: 'I' (Self) ki nenu unnanu ane spruha (consciousness) untundi. Kani Body ki adi brathikunna ani theliyadu (It doesn't know), endukante adi just oka material (koyya bomma lanti di).

2. Nenu brathakalankuntunna: Nenu ('I') brathakalani anukuntunnanu, daanikosam Body ni vaadukuntunnanu. Memiddaram (I & Body) kalisi untam. Decision theeskunedi nenu, panicheseedi naa body.

STEP 03: Happiness vs Physical Facilities
3. ‘I’ want to live in continuous happiness. For any happiness, the body needs physical facilities:
  • For the ‘I’ to enjoy the feelings of strength and health, my body needs food.
  • To get the feeling of protection, my body needs clothing and shelter.
  • For right utilization of the body, ‘I’ need various instruments and equipment. With the help of these, my body can make me feel happy.

🏮 'I' kosam Body ki Em Kavali?

'I' ki continuous happiness kavali. Ala happiness ravadaniki Body lopalnunchi co-operate cheyali. Daanikosam body ki 3 vasthuvulu (Physical facilities) kavali:

1. Food (Tindi): 'I' healthy ga, strong ga feel avvalante mundu body ki tindi kavali.

2. Shelter (Illu, Battalu): 'I' safe ga (protection) feel avvalante body ki battalu, illu kavali.

3. Instruments (Tools): Body ni correct ga vaadukuni, 'I' santhoshanga feel avvalante car, phone lanti equipment kavali.

STEP 04: The Complete Program
4. My (I) program for continuous happiness is to understand and to live in harmony all the four levels. Production, protection and right utilization of physical facilities is part of my program:
  • My complete program is to understand and live in harmony at all the four levels of my living. Ensuring physical facilities for the body is only a small part of the program. It consists of production, protection and right utilization of physical facilities.
  • Today, we measure our happiness in terms of materialistic things and want more and more physical facilities. However, these facilities do not ensure good health, happiness or the right understanding in ‘I’. therefore, we should focus on the complete program for ‘I’.

🏮 Naa (I) Life Goal Enti?

'I' yokka main goal enti ante.. srushti lo unna 4 levels lo Prasantham ga (Harmony) brathakadam. Ee physical facilities (vasthuvulu) thechhi pettukovadam anedi life lo oka chinna part matrame!

Kani ippudu janalu em chestunnaru? Kotha bike, kotha phone (Materialistic things) vaste vachhinde santhosham anukuntunnaru. Kani nijam enti ante.. vasthuvulu unnantha matrana health, happiness vaccheyavu. Anduke manam 'I' ki kavalsina complete happiness meeda focus cheyali.

STEP 05: I am the 'Seer' (Drasta)
5. I am the ‘Seer’, I am the ‘Doer’ and I am the ‘Enjoyer’:
  • I am the seer: I see through eyes. I is not the eye but the ‘I’ that sees or understands. Eye is just an instrument used by ‘I’. Seer also means the one that understands. Seer is also called ‘Drasta’.

🏮 I am the SEER (Chusevanni Nene - 'Drasta')

Kallu (Eyes) matrame chudavu. కళ్ళు అనేవి కేవలం రెండు కెమెరాలు మాత్రమే! (Instrument). ఆ కెమెరాల ద్వారా బయట ప్రపంచాన్ని చూసి, దాన్ని అర్థం చేసుకునేవాడిని (Seer) నేను ('I').

అందుకే కళ్ళు ఉండి కూడా ఒక్కోసారి మన ధ్యాస వేరే చోట ఉంటే ఎదురుగా ఉన్నది కనపడదు. ఎందుకంటే చూసే 'I' అక్కడ లేదు కాబట్టి! ఇలా చూసి అర్థం చేసుకునేవాడినే సంస్కృతంలో "ద్రష్ట" (Drasta) అంటారు.

STEP 06: I am the 'Doer' & 'Enjoyer'
  • I am the doer: Once I have seen and understand something, ‘I’ decide what I should/should not do. I take the help of my body parts to accomplish that task. Doer is also called ‘Karta’.
  • I am the Enjoyer: I use my body as an instrument to perform various activities. It is ‘I’ who enjoy the pleasure derived out of those activities. When I eat, mouth helps to chew and the tongue to taste, but ‘I’ enjoy the taste and flavor. Enjoyer is also called ‘Bhokta’.

🏮 I am the DOER ('Karta') & ENJOYER ('Bhokta')

💪 I am the DOER (Karta)

ఒక విషయాన్ని చూసి అర్థం చేసుకున్నాక.. ఏం చెయ్యాలి? ఏం చెయ్యకూడదు? అని డిసైడ్ చేసేది నేను ('I'). డిసైడ్ అయ్యాక నా చేతులతో/కాళ్ళతో (Body parts) ఆ పని చేయిస్తాను. ఇలా పని చేయించే (చేసే) నన్నే "కర్త" (Karta) అంటారు.

😋 I am the ENJOYER (Bhokta)

బాడీ తో నేను పనులు చేయిస్తాను, కానీ ఆ పనుల వల్ల వచ్చే మజా (Pleasure) ని అనుభవించేది నేను ('I'). నోటితో, పళ్ళతో బిర్యానీ నములుతాను.. కానీ ఆ టేస్ట్ ని, ఫ్లేవర్ ని ఎంజాయ్ చేసేది 'నేను'! ఇలా అనుభవించే నన్నే "భోక్త" (Bhokta) అంటారు.
