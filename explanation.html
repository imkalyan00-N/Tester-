🔌 HARDWARE INTERRUPTS

Step-by-Step Circuit Breakdown

STEP 01: The IR Line & Switches
An I/O device requests an interrupt by activating a bus-line called interrupt-request (IR).
  • A single interrupt request line may be used to serve n devices as depicted. All devices are connected to the line via switches to ground.

🏮 Interrupt Request (IR) Line Ante Enti?

Edaina I/O device CPU ki Interrupt pampalante, idi Interrupt-Request (IR) ane oka special bus-line (wire) ni on chestundi.

Oka motherboard lo Oka single IR line ki enni devices ayina (n devices) connect avvachu. Ee devices anni ela connect ayyi untayi ante... prathi device nunchi oka switch vachi ground ki (earth ki) connect ayyi untundi.

STEP 02: Inactive State (Vdd)
To request an interrupt, a device closes its associated switch. Thus, if all interrupt-request signals INTR1 to INTRn are inactive, that is, if all switches are open, the voltage on the interrupt-request line will be equal to Vdd.
  • This is the inactive state of the line.

🏮 Evaru Distrub Cheyanappudu em avtundi? (Inactive State)

Oka device ki interrupt kavalante adi tana switch ni 'Close' chestundi. Kani, okavela ye device CPU ni disturb cheyali anukovatledu anuko (ante INTR1 nunchi INTRn daka anni off lo unte), appudu anni switches 'Open' ga untayi.

Switches anni open ga unnappudu, aa interrupt-request line meeda Voltage Vdd (High voltage) untundi. Deenine Inactive State antaru. (Ante evaru CPU ni pilavatledu ani ardham).

STEP 03: Active State (Voltage Drops)
  • Since the closing of one or more switches will cause the line voltage to drop to 0, causing the interrupt request signal, INTR received by the processor to go to 1.

🏮 Signal Ela Veltundi? (Active State)

Okavela edo oka device leda ekkuva devices vallaki pani padi tana switch ni close chesayi anuko. Switch close avvagane, aa line voltage Vdd nunchi 0 ki drop aypotundi (Ground aypotundi).

Ila voltage 0 ki padipoyina ventane, CPU ki velthunna INTR signal '1' ga maripotundi (Active aypotundi). Appudu CPU ki alert vastundi "Oho, evaro nannu pilustunnaru" ani!

STEP 04: The Logical OR
  • The value of INTR is the logical OR of the requests from individual devices, that is,
    INTR = INTR1 + ………+INTRn
    It is customary to use the complemented form, INTR, to name the interrupt request signal on the common line, because this signal is active when in the low-voltage state.

🏮 The Logical OR (+) & Active Low

Motham INTR signal anedi.. andaru individual devices pampiche chinna chinna signals (INTR1, INTR2...) yokka Logical OR (+) tho samanam. Ante ye okkadu signal pampina (+), total INTR active aypotundi.

General ga ee signal ni Complement form lo (INTR-bar / INTR' laga) rastharu. Endukante, voltage low (0) lo unte ne kada signal active avtundi (pilustunattu ardham). Anduke deenni 'Active Low' signal antaru.

🏮 Circuit Gates (Open-collector / Open-drain)

Ee INTR line ni directly wire laaga kakunda, konni special gates dwara control chestaru. Bipolar circuits lo aythe vaatini "Open-collector" antaru, ade MOS circuits lo aythe "Open-drain" gates antaru.
