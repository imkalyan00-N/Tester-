HAPPINESS & PROSPERITY

Basic Human Aspirations (Module 3)

STEP 01: Happiness (Sukh)
Module 3: Continuous Happiness and Prosperity – the Basic Human Aspirations

3.1 Happiness (Sukh): Happiness may be defined as being in harmony/synergy in the state/ situation that I live in. “A state or situation in which I live, if there is harmony in it then I like to be in that state / situation. The state of liking is happiness.” It is a holistic and all-encompassing state of the mind that creates inner harmony.

🏮 Asalu 'Happiness' (Santhosham) ante enti?

Santhosham (Sukh) ante pedda secret em ledu. "Nenu unde situation lo elanti godavalu, tensions lekunda Prasantham ga (harmony/synergy) unte.. nenu aa situation ni ishtapaduthanu (liking)."

Ila manam ishtapade (like chese) state ne Happiness antaru. Idi edho vasthuvu kaadu, mana mind lopalnunchi vache oka inner harmony (prasanthatha).

Mass Example: Intlo family tho kurchuni, elanti problems lekunda navvukuntu time spend chestunnav anuko.. adi neeku ishtam (you like it). Aa state of liking eh Happiness!
STEP 02: Prosperity vs Wealth
3.2 Prosperity: The feeling of having or making available more than required physical facilities is prosperity. Almost all of us feel that wealth alone means prosperity and try to explain this phenomenon on this nonexistent or half fact. We are trying to achieve happiness and prosperity by maximizing accumulation and consumption of physical facilities. It is becoming anti-ecological and anti-people, and threatening the human survival itself.

🏮 Prosperity (Samrudhi) vs Wealth (Dabbu)

Prosperity (Samrudhi) ante "Naku kavalsina avasarala kante ekkuva Physical facilities (vasthuvulu/dabbu) naa daggara unnayi" ane oka feeling!

Kani janalu bhramapadedi entante: Wealth (Dabbu) = Prosperity anukuntaru. Dabbulu, vasthuvulu pichipichiga koodabettukuni (accumulation), viparithanga vaadesthu (consumption) unte santhosham vastundi anukuntaru. Kani deeni valla nature ki (anti-ecological), manushulaki (anti-people) chala nastam vastundi, asalu manugadake (survival) pramadham vastundi.

STEP 03: 2 Rules of Prosperity
For prosperity, two things are required-For prosperity, two things are required:
(1) Identification of the required quantity of physical facilities.
(2) Ensuring availability / production of more than required physical facilities.We can be prosperous only if there is a limit to the need for physical facilities. If there is no limit what so ever be the availability the feeling of prosperity cannot be assured.

Secondly, just assessing the need is not enough. We need to be able to produce or make available more than the perceived need.

🏮 Prosperity kavalante ee 2 Rules Follow Avvali

Nijamaina Samrudhi (Prosperity) feeling ravalante rendu theliyali:

Rule 1: Naku asalu Entha Kavali? (Identify required quantity). Mundu naku asalu enni vasthuvulu kavalo oka Limit pettukovali. Limit lekapothe prapancham antha icchina neeku saripodu, prosperity feeling radu.

Rule 2: Kavalasina dani kante ekkuva produce cheyadam! Naku kavalsina limit ento thelisaka, daani kante ekkuva (more than required) theచ్చుకుని ready ga pettukovali.

STEP 04: The 3 Requirements
3.3 The requirements to fulfil basic human aspirations
Our basic aspirations are happiness (mutual fulfilment) and prosperity (mutual prosperity). Happiness is ensured by the relationships with other human beings and prosperity is ensured by working on physical facilities.
 Right Understanding: Understanding the harmony at four levels of living from self to existence. This refers to higher order human skills – the need to learn and utilize our intelligence most effectively
 Good Relationships: This refers to the interpersonal relationships that a person builds in his or her life – at home, at the workplace and in society.
 Physical Facilities: This includes the physiological needs of individuals and indicates the necessities as well as the comforts of life. It means the feeling of having or being able to have more physical facilities than is needed.

🏮 Basic Goals theeralante em kavali? (The 3 Pillars)

Mana basic goals Happiness & Prosperity. Ee rendu theeralante manaki 3 mukhyamaina pillars kavali:

  • 1. Right Understanding (Sarayina Avagahana): Manam nunchi srushti daka 4 levels lo harmony ni ardam cheskovadam. Mana intelligence ni effective ga vadukovadam.
  • 2. Good Relationships: Manushulathounde relationships valle manaki 'Happiness' vastundi. Intlo, aphis lo, society lo manchi relations maintain cheyadam.
  • 3. Physical Facilities: Ivi unteనే manaki 'Prosperity' vastundi. Mana basic needs (tindi, battalu, comforts) kavalasina dani kante ekkuva unnayi ane feeling kalagadam.
STEP 05: Power of Right Understanding
In order to resolve the issues in human relationships, we need to understand them first, and this would come from ‘right understanding of relationship’. Similarly in order to be prosperous and to enrich nature, we need to have the ‘right understanding’. The ‘right understanding’ will enable us to work out our requirements for physical facilities and hence correctly distinguish the difference between wealth and prosperity. With nature as well, we need to understand the harmony in nature, and how we can complement this harmony.

🏮 Right Understanding Valla Vache Use Enti?

Annitikante Top and important ayindi 'Right Understanding' ae! Endukante:

Nee relationships lo unna problems (godavalu) solve cheyalante idi kavali.
Asalu Wealth (dabbu) ki Prosperity (Samrudhi) ki unde Theda (Difference) telusukovalante idi kavali.
Neku life lo exactly enni physical facilities kavalo (limit) telusukovalante idi kavali.
Last ki, Nature (Prakruthi) ni paadu cheyakunda nature tho manam ela brathakalo theliyalanukunna kooda 'Right understanding' eh mukhyam.
