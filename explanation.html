STEP 01: The Levels of Human Being
MODULE 1: Understanding Human being as the co-existence of the self and the body

2.1 Understand human being
  • Human beings are complex creations of the elements of nature.
  • Physically they are a composition of minerals andwater.
  • At the second level human beings are capable of movement and the ability to respond tostimuli.
  • At the third level human beings are thinking being who have intellect andemotions.
  • At the highest-level human beings have a spiritual aspect.

🏮 Manishi ante Evaru? (4 Levels of Human)

Manishi anevadu nature srustinchina oka athi pedda complex creation. Manam basic ga 4 levels lo untam:

1. Physical Level: Manam just konni minerals mariyu neetitho (water) tayarayina oka roopam.
2. Movement Level: Kurchovekkada kurchokunda kadalagalam, evadaina gillithe ventane react avvagalige shakti (respond to stimuli) manakundi.
3. Thinking Level: Jantuvulaki lekunna.. manaki intellect (burra), emotions (felligs) unnayi.
4. Highest Level: Annitikante paina manaki aatmika shakti (Spiritual aspect) undi.

🏮 Suvidha (Vasaluthulu - Only for Body)

Suvidha ante physical comforts (vasaluthulu) kosam chuskovadam. Idi purthiga Body ki sambandhinchindi.

Mana body ki eppudaina oka range comfort ki alavatu ayyindante, inka body aa range lone undali anukuntundi, thagganivvadu.
Example: Nuvvu fan gaali ki alavatupaddav anuko, cooler kavalipistundi. Cooler unte, AC kavalipistundi.

STEP 05: Sukh (Happiness)
Sukh:
The beauty of Sukh is that it is holistic and all encompassing state of the mind that creates inner harmony.
By nature, man is fond off comfort and happiness so he goes on making desires and ambitions one after the other to enjoy more in life. To lead a comfortable accumulates many facilities, so that his life may become full of comfort and happiness. depends upon our thinking, so many times we are surrounded by materialistic possessions but we feel unsatisfied. People think that their happiness depends upon Suvidha (facilities) but is it not so; happiness depends upon our thinking or our mentalsatisfaction.

🏮 Sukh (Santhosham - Only for 'I')

Sukh ante lopala mind lo unde santhosham / prasanthatha (Inner harmony). Idi purthiga 'I' (Self) ki sambandhinchindi.

Nature param ga manishiki comforts, happiness kavali. Anduke vadu life enjoy cheyadaniki oka korika tarvata inko korika penchukuntu poyi.. AC lu, car lu anni thechhi petkuntadu.
Kani janalu bhramapadedi entante: 'Suvidha' (Vasthuvulu) unte 'Sukh' (Santhosham) vachestundi ani! Idi utter flop! Enni vasthuvulu (materialistic possessions) nee chuttu unna sare nuvvu lopala unsatisfied ga feel avvachu.

Endukante, Santhosham anedi vasthuvula (Suvidha) meeda depend avvadu.. adi mana Alochana (Thinking) mariyu Mental satisfaction meeda matrame depend ayi untundi!
